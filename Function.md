### Function 
>A [[Function]] $f$ from a [[set]] $X$ to a [[set]] $Y$ assigns to each [[element]] of $X$  exactly one 
>[[element]] in $Y$. $X$ is called the [[domain]], and $Y$ the [[codomain]] of $f$.
