### Power set
>For the [[set]] $M:$ 
>$\mathcal{P}(M) = \{ A | A\subseteq M \}$ 

"the [[Power set]] of a [[set]] $A$ contains all [[set]]s that are [[contains|contained]] by $A$, i.e $S_{i}\subseteq A$"
