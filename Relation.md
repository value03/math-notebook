### Relation
for [[set]]s $X$ and $Y$, every [[subset]] $p\subseteq X\times Y$ is a (**unary or binary**) [[Relation]] **on** $X\times Y$. The Graph $G_f$ of **function** $f:X\to Y$ is a [[Relation]] on $X\times Y$ with the condition that for every
[[element]] $x$ of $X$, there's exactly one $y\in Y$.
	for $X=Y$, we say that $p$ is a [[relation]] **on** $X$ 
**examples**: [[divisibility]], [[modular congruence]]
