### restriction
For a [[Function]] $f:X\rightarrow Y$, the [[subset]] $A\subseteq X$ forms a new [[function]] from [[set]] $A$ to $Y$  : $$
f|_A:\bigg\{
\begin{equation}
\begin{aligned}
&A\to Y \\
&x \mapsto f(x) 
\end{aligned}
\end{equation}
$$ which we call the **restriction** of $f$
